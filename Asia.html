Let's take a trip to Asia!

Asia is not only Earth’s largest continent but also its youngest and structurally most-complicated one. Asia has both the highest and the lowest points on the surface of Earth, has the longest coastline of any continent, is subject overall to the world’s widest climatic extremes, and, consequently, produces the most varied forms of vegetation and animal life on Earth. Some animals found in Asia are - Malayan tapir, Giant Panda, Asian Elephant, J apanese Maquae, etc.Chilli Crab in Singapore, Khantoke Dinner in Thailand, Curry Feast in Sri Lanka, Kimchi in Korea are the types of food eaten in different contried of Asia. The tallest building and bridge is in Asia. The longest bullet train is also in Asia. Ayurveda and Kung Fu also originated in Asia.
